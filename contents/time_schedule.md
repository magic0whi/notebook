# Weekly Schedule & Daily Routines

## The Weekly Theme Flow
- **Monday:** NixOS & Development (Home)
- **Tuesday:** Math & Competitive Programming (Home)
- **Wednesday: Downtown Day (Starbucks Work & Bento Restock)**
- **Thursday:** NixOS & Development (Home)
- **Friday:** Math, Competitive Programming & Deltarune (Home)
- **Saturday:** Piano, Gaming, or Flex Deep Work (Home - avoiding crowds)
- **Sunday:** Rest, Reset, and Beancount Maintenance (Home)

---

## Standard Home Day (Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri, Sat, Sun)
*Note: Skip the 04:20 stock trading on Saturday and Sunday mornings.*

| Time | Activity | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **04:20 - 04:30** | **Wake Up & Trading** | Buy 10 stocks at the US market close. |
| **04:30 - 04:45** | **Brew Coffee** | Craft morning coffee with milk and syrups. |
| **04:45 - 05:45** | **Walk, Farm & Mobile Anki** | Walk (2-3 km/h), grind reward jobs, flag TTS Anki cards. |
| **05:45 - 07:00** | **Desktop Anki & TTS** | Correct flagged TTS cards at the computer. |
| **07:00 - 08:00** | **Breakfast & Deep Clean** | Eat, wash breakfast tableware, clean the coffee machine. Empty the robot vacuum, mop, and vacuum. |
| **08:00 - 12:00** | **Deep Work Block 1** | Focus on the day's theme (NixOS, Math, or Competitive Programming). |
| **12:00 - 13:00** | **Lunch & Quick Clean** | Eat, wash lunch tableware, and disconnect. |
| **13:00 - 15:00** | **Deep Work Block 2** | Continue the day's core theme. |
| **15:00 - 16:30** | **Beancount Kickstart** | Stitch receipts via LocalSend, process multi-country ledger. |
| **16:30 - 17:30** | **Piano Practice** | Transition brain from logical thinking to creative motor skills. |
| **17:30 - 18:00** | **Dinner & Quick Clean** | Eat and wash dinner tableware. *(On Fridays/Weekends, play Deltarune instead of Piano, push dinner slightly later).* |
| **18:00 - 19:00** | **Shower & Wind Down** | Warm shower to drop core temperature. No screens. |
| **19:00** | **Sleep** | Lights out. |

## Mid-Week Downtown Day (Wednesday)

| Time | Activity | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **04:20 - 04:30** | **Wake Up & Trading** | Buy 10 stocks at the US market close. |
| **04:30 - 05:45** | **Coffee, Walk & Farm** | Brew coffee, walk, do app tasks, and flag Anki cards. |
| **05:45 - 06:45** | **Desktop Anki & TTS** | Correct flagged TTS cards at the computer. |
| **06:45 - 07:15** | **Breakfast & Deep Clean** | Eat, wash breakfast tableware, clean the coffee machine, empty the vacuum, pack MacBook. |
| **07:15 - 08:15** | **Commute (Outbound)** | Catch the ~7:15 AM bus and subway to the plaza. |
| **08:15 - 12:00** | **Focus Block 1 (Starbucks)** | Settle into a quiet cafe. Work on NixOS or competitive programming. |
| **12:00 - 13:00** | **FamilyMart Restock** | Walk to FamilyMart before the lunch rush. Eat bento and buy stock for Thu/Fri. |
| **13:00 - 15:30** | **Focus Block 2 (Starbucks)** | Second deep work session. |
| **15:30 - 16:30** | **Beancount Kickstart** | Transfer receipts via LocalSend, stitch images, log transactions. |
| **16:30 - 17:30** | **Commute (Return)** | Pack up at 16:30 to catch the ~17:15 bus, beating the 18:00 rush hour. |
| **17:30 - 18:00** | **Unpack, Dinner & Clean** | Put new bentos in fridge, eat dinner, and wash tableware. |
| **18:00 - 19:00** | **Shower & Wind Down** | Warm shower to prepare for sleep. |
| **19:00** | **Sleep** | Lights out. |
